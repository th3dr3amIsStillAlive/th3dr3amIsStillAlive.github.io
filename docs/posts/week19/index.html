As the 19th week of my poker journey folds, I find myself in a phase of diligent progress and introspective learning. Embracing the 100k challenge, my bankroll has reached a healthy $15,497.00.

The Grind: Focusing on NL500

This week’s poker grind was concise yet impactful. On Monday, an hour at the NL500 tables brought in a $1,265 profit, while Wednesday’s mixed session of NL500 and NL500 ante games padded the bankroll with an additional $1,815. Can’t complain about the first few hands shotting at the next stake.

The Study: Sharpening Skills

Finding this balance between grinding and theoretical study is crucial for long-term growth and success in poker.
