Week 15: Merry Christmas.

The ups and downs of NL200

In the spirit of the holiday season, this week’s poker journey was akin to a rollercoaster ride through a winter wonderland of variance. Basically, every session was quite swingy. I think overall I rate my execution quite high. However, I did take a few sub-optimal lines in some spots that I haven’t studied much due to the low frequency of occurrence, and hence they haven’t been prioritised during study sessions.

Reflections

The most significant takeaway from this week is the importance of solver work and to spend more time studying. So, I will try to focus more on that going forward. Another thing is that I am getting really annoyed with playing on untracked sites where I can’t use pokertracker. I really miss having concrete stats on myself and not having to screenshot every hand that I want to review. I do have like 20% of my volume on GG, so I at least get some hands into my database.
